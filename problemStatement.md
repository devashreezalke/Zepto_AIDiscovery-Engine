# Problem Statement: Zepto AI-Powered Discovery Engine

## Context

**Role:** Product Manager, Growth Team at Zepto

Zepto is a quick-commerce platform that has successfully embedded itself into users' weekly shopping routines. Users regularly place recurring orders for:
- Groceries
- Snacks & beverages
- Household essentials

---

## The Problem

Over time, **shopping behavior becomes highly repetitive**. Users tend to purchase the same set of products repeatedly and rarely explore new categories available on the platform.

This creates a significant growth bottleneck: the platform has a broad catalog across many categories, yet a large portion of Monthly Active Customers remain siloed within their initial shopping habits.

### Strategic Goal

> **Increase the percentage of Monthly Active Customers (MACs) who purchase products from at least one new category every month.**

### Illustrative Examples

| Current Behavior | Desired Expansion |
|---|---|
| User buys groceries | Starts buying pet supplies |
| User buys snacks | Starts buying personal care products |
| User buys household essentials | Starts buying baby products |

---

## What We Need to Understand

To drive category expansion, we must first understand the behavioral and experiential barriers. Key research questions include:

1. **Habit Formation** — Why do users repeatedly buy from the same categories?
2. **Discovery Barriers** — What prevents users from exploring new categories?
3. **Current Discovery Mechanisms** — How do users discover products today?
4. **Role of Habits** — What role do habits play in shopping behavior?
5. **Information Needs** — What information do users need before trying a new category?
6. **Pain Points** — What frustrations emerge repeatedly?
7. **User Segmentation** — Which user segments are more likely to experiment with new categories?
8. **Unmet Needs** — What unmet needs emerge consistently across user discussions?

---

## Part 1: Build an AI-Powered Discovery Engine

To answer these questions at scale, we need an **AI-powered system** that analyzes user-generated feedback from multiple sources.

### Data Sources

The discovery engine will ingest and analyze signals from:

- 📱 **App Store reviews** (iOS)
- 🤖 **Play Store reviews** (Android)
- 💬 **Reddit discussions**
- 🗣️ **Community forums**
- 📣 **Social media conversations**
- ⭐ **Product reviews**
- 🛵 **Quick-commerce discussions**

### Engine Responsibilities

The AI Discovery Engine must demonstrate:

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **Data Gathering** | How the workflow collects data across all sources |
| **Theme Identification** | How recurring themes and patterns are surfaced |
| **Insight Generation** | How actionable insights are derived from raw data |
| **Quality Validation** | How the quality and reliability of insights are validated |

---

## Success Criteria

A successful discovery engine will enable the Growth Team to:

- Identify **specific, evidence-backed barriers** to category exploration
- Surface **underserved user segments** with higher experimentation potential
- Generate **actionable product and marketing recommendations** to nudge cross-category discovery
- Validate insights with enough rigor to inform roadmap prioritization

---

## Scope

This document covers **Part 1** of the broader growth initiative — establishing the AI-powered data collection and insight-generation foundation before designing interventions or experiments.
